    • About Project
    MyDoc is a dedicated virtual-consultation platform for doctors to help their patients Find doctors from all specialties like General Physicians, Gynaecologists, Dermatologists etc.. Bridging the gap between the Doctors and Patients virtually. Booking appointments made easy and get prescriptions from the wide range of Doctors available in the platform.
    Project type & Involvement
    Concept - UI UX.
    Deliverables
    Personas and user stories, user flow diagrams, conceptual wireframes, a prototype app, and a clean and modern visual design.
    Problems from previous consultation apps
    Complicated Appointment booking page.
    Adding payment options.
    Emergency Services.
    Viewing past appointments & prescription downloading.
    Appointment remainder and schedule remainder..
